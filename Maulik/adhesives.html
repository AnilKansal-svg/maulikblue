High-Performance Adhesive & Sealant Solutions for a Sustainable Future

At MAULIK, whether serving construction, automotive, packaging, or other industries, our adhesive and sealant technologies reflect our commitment to sustainable materials innovation. We deliver high-performance solutions that prioritize environmental responsibility and social impact—helping customers meet their performance goals while advancing sustainability.

Adhesives

MAULIK is a worldwide leading manufacturer of adhesive raw materials. Our water-based polymer dispersions, polymer resins, UV curable systems and our additives offer excellent solutions to produce high-performance adhesives for various applications. The industry is faced with constantly growing technical requirements. We strive to reliably support our partners with our expertise and our innovative raw materials that enable the development of safe and sustainable adhesives.

Sealants

MAULIK offers acrylic polymer dispersions and technical know-how to achieve formulations for gunnable sealants and construction adhesives that are exceptionally resilient, cost-effective and durable, as well as easy-to-use and clean. Our quality binders are key when it comes to closing gaps, sticking elements to different substrates, filling and isolating. At the same time providing high-quality, sustainable and aesthetically pleasing finishes in all kinds of demanding sealant applications.
