Advanced Solutions for Packaging, Print & Paper Industries

Whether it's maintaining food freshness, enhancing packaging convenience, or optimizing cost-efficiency, we understand the unique demands of your industry. Our innovation efforts are strategically aligned to deliver these core values, providing advanced and sustainable solutions tailored for the packaging, printing, and paper sectors.

Comprehensive Solutions for All Your Packaging Requirements

Maulik provides the packaging industry with a comprehensive portfolio of high-performance materials and advanced solutions. Whether it's food and beverages, cosmetics, or pharmaceutical products, Maulik delivers tailored packaging technologies designed to protect, preserve, and present products effectively across diverse applications.

High-Performance Technologies for Printing Inks and Coatings

Maulik develops advanced chemical solutions to deliver high-quality products for the printing and packaging industry. Our portfolio includes water-based binders and resins for printing inks, overprint varnishes, and functional packaging coatings—such as barrier and heat-seal technologies—designed for use in food packaging, flexible packaging, paper, paperboard, corrugated board, and labels.

Innovative Solutions for Enhanced Paper and Board Production

Maulik delivers a comprehensive range of specialized chemical solutions for the pulp and paper industry, precisely formulated to address the distinct needs of packaging, printing, writing, and tissue paper manufacturers. Prioritizing sustainability, innovation, and process excellence, we enable our customers to enhance product quality, maximize operational efficiency, and reduce environmental impact.
