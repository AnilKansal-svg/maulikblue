Textile, Leather & Footwear Solutions

At MAULIK, we deliver advanced material and chemical solutions across the textile, leather, and footwear value chains. Our expertise spans performance-enhancing footwear materials, sustainable textile auxiliaries, and next-generation soft-touch coatings. Through innovation, process integration, and a strong focus on sustainability, we empower manufacturers to meet evolving market demands in functionality, design, and regulatory compliance.

Footwear Materials

MAULIK has been a trusted partner in the global footwear industry, offering customized high-performance materials engineered for all types of footwear and sole systems. Our advanced polyurethane systems—developed for superior durability, comfort, and design flexibility—enable manufacturers to deliver lightweight, energy-efficient, and ergonomically optimized footwear.

Textile Chemicals

MAULIK has established itself as a reliable partner from fiber to finished fabric. Our comprehensive portfolio of textile auxiliaries and specialty chemicals supports sustainable processing, colorfastness, functional finishes, and performance enhancements across apparel, home textiles, and industrial fabrics.

Leather Solutions

MAULIK develops and commercializes advanced soft-touch coating technologies specifically engineered for highly flexible leather surfaces. Designed for use in footwear, automotive interiors, and premium leather goods, these coatings enhance tactile quality, durability, and aesthetic appeal.

Our solutions set new benchmarks in design flexibility, surface customization, and process efficiency—enabling manufacturers to achieve distinctive finishes and superior performance. With a strong focus on innovation and sustainability, MAULIK's coating systems help elevate leather products to meet evolving consumer and industry expectations.

Leather Auxiliaries

Leather auxiliaries are performance-enhancing chemical agents formulated to optimize each stage of leather processing—from beamhouse to finishing. These include surfactants, emulsifiers, degreasers, dispersing agents, wetting agents, fatliquors, and retanning aids designed to ensure uniform penetration, improved chemical reactivity, enhanced softness, and controlled surface finish.

Textile Effects

Textile effects are specialized chemical formulations designed to enhance the aesthetic and functional properties of fabrics. Our range includes softeners, water repellents, flame retardants, and antimicrobial treatments that improve fabric performance, durability, and sustainability while meeting the highest industry standards.
