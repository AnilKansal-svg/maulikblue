Paints and Coating

Our portfolio encompasses advanced solutions across key application areas, including marine antifouling to protect vessels from biofouling and corrosion, dry-film protection for long-lasting microbial resistance in paints and plasters, and plant hygiene technologies to control contamination and ensure operational efficiency. These innovations are supported by slow-release systems and inert-material-based formulations, delivering high performance, sustainability, and regulatory compliance across industrial and architectural coatings.

Marine Antifouling

Our advanced marine antifouling solutions protect surfaces in constant seawater contact—such as ship hulls and nets—from hard and soft-fouling organisms. Designed for long-term effectiveness, they prevent biofouling and corrosion, enhance fuel efficiency, and ensure lasting performance in marine coatings.

Dry-Film Protection

Our dry-film preservatives provide broad-spectrum, long-lasting protection against fungi, yeast, and algae, preventing surface defacement and enhancing coating durability. Available in liquid and powder forms, they offer color stability, zero VOCs, high solubility, and resistance to temperature and pH changes. Designed for low-leaching and easy formulation, our diverse portfolio supports a wide range of performance and regulatory needs across coating applications.

Plant Hygiene

Inadequate industrial hygiene can cause microbial contamination, leading to equipment damage, reduced efficiency, and product quality issues. Our experts conduct microbial audits to identify contamination sources and implement best practices, helping prevent costly downtime and ensure reliable production.

Paints and Plaster

We provide specialized antimicrobial solutions and performance additives for industrial applications, with a strong focus on paint and plaster protection. Our technologies offer preservation, along with advanced microbial control innovations like slow-release systems and inert-material-based solutions—enhancing durability, efficiency, and sustainability across applications.
